Matthew Cuschieri is a designer and artist from New Jersey. He holds a BFA from RISD studying graphic design & painting and concentrating in drawing.

Extended portfolio contact matthewdcuschieri[@]gmail.com

Previously he worked for Siegel+Gale,the RISD Design Guild, Johnson & Johnson Brand Design Studio, Brian Belott and Ben La Rocco as a studio intern. He has collaborated with RISD Marcomm. He has been invited to attend Anderson Ranch Arts Center, was featured in New American Paintings no. 152, the College Hill Independent, and exhibited in the Gelman Gallery (Memories and How We Hold Them) and Online Playroom (Love is in the Air)."

Pond, 2023

Using the Awards' symbol of the anvil to inform the type and composition of the poster

With the anvil as a structural element the rest of the poster plays off the typical cartoony 'anvil fall.' When the anvil is larger, the text shrinks and the composition then changes. The custom type also plays off J&J's inhouse typeface, a requirement of the prompt. The typeface can easily stretch and squash. Its form is made through broken down shapes found in the anvil.

Commencement

With it being the first year of the new RISD identity we had to create a system that introduced new iconic elements to match with RISD’s new blue. The identity is inspired by bead mazes and utlizes the school’s diverse majors to inform the bead design. Each element of the system also carries the green wire as an identifying system color.

Collaborated with Joyce Ho and RISD Media to create a fun identity based around bead maze toys.
