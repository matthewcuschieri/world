Matthew Cuschieri is a designer and artist interested in infrastructure. He is currently pursuing a double major BFA in Graphic Design and Painting at RISD. Previously he worked for Brian Belott and Ben La Rocco as a studio intern.

He was recently invited to attend Anderson Ranch Arts Center, featured in New American Paintings no. 152, the College Hill Independent, and exhibited in the Gelman Gallery(Memories and How We Hold Them) and Online Playroom (Love is in the Air).
